# Planner — Checklist

Before handing off a proposal, confirm:

- [ ] Proposed battle is strictly binary (exactly two subjects).
- [ ] Category is within the allowed topic space (technology, products, pop
      culture) — never sport, politics, religion.
- [ ] Proposed pair does not already exist in `comparisons.ts` or
      `output/battles/`.
- [ ] A `title` and a one-line `rationale` are included.
- [ ] No battle content (script/caption/hashtags/prompts) has been written —
      that's the Battle Designer's job.
- [ ] The proposal was generated deterministically (same category always
      produces the same output) — no LLM, no randomness.
